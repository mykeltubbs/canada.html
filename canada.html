History

Canada, known for it's spacious land and natural resources, became a self governing dominion in 1867. Canada gained independence from Britain in 1931 and created the Canada Act in 1982 formally gaining independence from the UK. Canada rivals the United States in it's economic and technological advancements.

Geography

Location

Canada is northern North America bordering the North Atlantic Ocean on the east, North Pacific Ocean on the west, the Arctic Ocean on the north, and North Of United States boarder.

Terrain

Mostly plains with mountains in the west and lowlands in the southeast.

Government

Government Type

Federal parliamentary democracy (Parliament of Canada) under a constitutional monarchy. A commonwealth realm, federal and state authorities, responsibilities regulated in constitution.

Government Setup

  • Executive Branch
  • Judicial Branch
  • Legislative Branch

Chief of Staff

Queen Elizabeth

Head of Government

Prime Minister Justin Pierre James

Cabinet

Federal Ministry chosen by the prime minister usually from among members of his/her own party sitting in Parliament

National Anthem

Canada's nationl anthem is "O Canada" and was written by Adolphe-Basile Routhier, Robert Stanley Weir and Calixa Lavallee. The anthem was adopted in 1980 and served as an unofficial anthem before it was officially adopted.
